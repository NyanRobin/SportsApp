Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the FieldSync app and tap "Register" to create a new account. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password.

How do I join a team?

You can join a team by entering a team code provided by your coach or team manager, or by accepting a team invitation.

How do I sync my data across devices?

FieldSync automatically syncs your data to the cloud. Simply log in with the same account on any device to access your data.

Can I export my statistics?

Yes! You can export your statistics and reports from the Statistics section of the app.

How do I report a bug?

Please report bugs through our GitHub Issues page or contact support directly.
